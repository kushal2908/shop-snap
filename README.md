## 🚀 The Problem

F-commerce sellers in Bangladesh often face three major hurdles:

1. **The iPhone Trap:** `.HEIC` photos are hard to upload and often look blurry after Facebook's aggressive compression.
2. **The Branding Burnout:** Manually watermarking 50+ images in Canva or PicsArt takes hours.
3. **Data Friction:** High-res photos (4MB+) are too heavy for customers browsing on 4G mobile networks (GP/Robi).

## ✨ Features

- **Instant HEIC Conversion:** Drop Apple `.HEIC` files and get optimized `.JPG`s instantly.
- **Smart "Squeezer" Compression:** Automatically resizes to **1080px (FB/IG Gold Standard)** and compresses files to **<600KB** without losing visible sharpness.
- **Bulk Watermarking:** Upload your shop logo once; apply it to all photos with adjustable opacity and positioning.
- **Privacy-First (WASM):** All processing happens in the **browser**. Your product photos never touch a server.
- **Batch Export:** Download your entire processed collection in one organized `.ZIP` file.
